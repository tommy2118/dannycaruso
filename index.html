About Me

A 2021 Holy Trinity Catholic High School graduate, I came to Waldorf University on a Creative Writing Scholarship. Inspired by my older brother Tommy's theatrical pursuits, I discovered my passion for performance through dinner theatre and musicals at HTC under Stewart Beyer's direction. Now, I'm pursuing both writing and acting, letting both creative paths shape my journey.
